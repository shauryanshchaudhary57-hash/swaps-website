Two ways to swap it

Same black sole in both. Tap each to see it detach, then tell us which you'd rather have when you join the waitlist.

MECH-01

Whole strap detaches

Simple, one piece — but the whole strap (shape included) is what you're replacing.

MECH-02

Overlay swaps, base stays

The base is fused to the sole permanently. Only the thin overlay on top ever comes off.
